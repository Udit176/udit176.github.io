ABOUT ME

WELCOME

Hello! My name is Udit Ekansh. I'm currently pursuing my Master's in Autonomy at Purdue University, USA. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on Mechatronics and Automation from MIT Art, Design, and Technology University, India (2023) Prior to joining Purdue, I worked at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay as a research assistant under the guidance of Professor Leena Vachhani. My work at IIT focused on autonomous navigation in highly stochastic 3D underwater environments. My current research interests include offline reinforcement learning, coordinated planning and control, and their applications to autonomous and connected systems.
