Graduate Student in Autonomy

I am a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Autonomy at Purdue University. My research interests include offline reinforcement learning, coordinated planning and control, and applications of autonomous and connected systems.

Prior to Purdue, I worked at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, focusing on autonomous navigation in stochastic 3D underwater environments under the guidance of Professor Leena Vachhani. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Mechatronics and Automation from the MIT Art, Design, and Technology University.
